PYRAMID Project Dashboard

This multicenter prospective study investigates long-term complications, risk factors, and biological mechanisms in survivors of acute respiratory failure (ARF) after ICU admission. It combines clinical data with transcriptomic, immune-response, and microbiome analyses to identify patient phenotypes and develop predictive models using AI. Participants undergo follow-up visits up to one year after discharge, including functional, cognitive, and quality-of-life assessments. The ultimate goal is to guide precision medicine and propose standardized care pathways for this under-recognized patient population.

Phages Project Dashboard

This study investigates a personalized therapy combining bacteriophage cocktails with ceftolozane/tazobactam to treat P. aeruginosa ventilator‑associated pneumonia, particularly in carbapenem‑resistant cases. The main objective is to evaluate the safety and efficacy of this phage‑antibiotic approach, focusing on bacterial clearance and lung tissue outcomes. Secondary objectives include studying resistance evolution, biofilm disruption, microbiota effects, and safety indicators such as PK/PD and inflammatory responses. The project also examines how phages and antibiotics interact within biofilms and endotracheal tubes, providing mechanistic insights into treatment performance. Overall, the study aims to support phage‑based therapies as effective and microbiota‑sparing alternatives for severe resistant infections.

📚 How to add a new dashboard

1

Copy the template

Duplicate general_code/redcap_dashboard.ipynb into your project folder.

2

Edit Cell 1

Set PROJECT_KEY, title, and the CHARTS list. That's the only part you need to change.

3

Run all cells

The notebook fetches data from REDCap, renders charts, and exports the HTML file.

4

Commit & push

Push the HTML documents to GitHub. The live page updates instantly.
